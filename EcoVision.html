Key Impacts Across Pakistan

From the mountains to the coast, climate signals are accelerating.

Rising Heatwaves

Longer, hotter summers increase health risks and energy demand.

Smog & Air Quality

Pollution + weather patterns create hazardous winter smog in cities.

Water Stress

Variable rainfall & overuse strain rivers, aquifers, and agriculture.

Food Security

Heat & floods cut yields for wheat, rice, cotton, and fruits.

Glacier Retreat

Changing snowfall & warming threaten long-term water supply.

Coastal Risks

Sea-level rise and storms threaten Sindh's coastal communities.

Changing Climate Scenes

Floods, drought, heat — and the resilience of people.

What You Can Do

Small steps at home. Big change together.

Save Energy

Use LEDs, unplug idle chargers, set efficient AC temperatures.

Conserve Water

Fix leaks, take shorter showers, reuse greywater safely.

Reduce Waste

Carry a bottle & bag, cut single-use plastic, compost organics.

Plant and Protect

Join local tree drives, protect green belts & mangroves.

Stay Informed • Get Involved

Collaborate with students, NGOs, and communities across Pakistan.
